Albino Bear Entertainment

Albino Bear Entertainment is a small group. Dedicated to podcasting a wide spectrum of nerd, and geek hobbies/culture. While representing the local north-west regions identity. Based in British Coumbia Canada, with "the kermode spirit bear" as our group symbol of choice. The kermode spirit bear is part of the bear sancturay, only found in the province of B.C. Pod casts will be done on a semi-weekly regular basis.
